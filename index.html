WHAT WE OFFER

Sized for every roof under the Philippine sun.

No one-size-fits-all kits. We engineer around how you actually use power — from a two-aircon home to a factory roof.

01

Net Metering

Generate your own solar power and send the excess back to the grid. Your meter runs backwards on sunny days, earning credits that slash your monthly electric bill.

GRID-TIED BILL CREDITS MERALCO-READY
02

Hybrid

Solar panels paired with battery storage so you stay powered through brownouts and after dark. Still grid-connected for net metering — the best of both worlds.

SOLAR + BATTERY BROWNOUT-PROOF NIGHT POWER
03

Grid

Large-scale rooftop and carport arrays wired directly to the grid. No battery cost, maximum daytime savings — ideal for commercial and industrial operations.
